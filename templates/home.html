Welcome to Anoteer

Your one‐stop app to manage projects, upload data items (texts or images), and annotate them with ease.

View Projects Create New Project
Create Projects

Start by organizing your work into projects. Each project can group together a set of related items for annotation.

Manage Data Items

Upload or create text and image files as “Data Items.” Each item belongs to a project and will be listed for annotation.

Annotate Easily

For each Data Item, use a simple form for annotations. Save and export your results seamlessly.

What Anoteer Will Do One Day

Today, Anoteer helps you organize projects, upload text/image items, and annotate them with checkboxes. In the near future, we plan to add:

  • Collaborative Annotation: Invite teammates to work on the same project simultaneously, with role‐based permissions.
  • Analytics Dashboard: Generate charts and summaries of your annotation metrics (e.g., counts, agreement rates).
  • Export & Integration: Export annotations as CSV/JSON, or integrate directly with other tools (e.g., machine learning pipelines).
